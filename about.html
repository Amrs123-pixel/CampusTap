CampusTap

CampusTap is a passionate and innovative project reimagining campus mobility through technology. Created as a thesis Project by students from the University of San Carlos, CampusTap aims to deliver seamless, cashless, and real-time shuttle solutions tailored for university communities.

Our Mission

To enhance campus transportation by providing smart, efficient, and user-friendly solutions that empower students and administrators alike. We believe in leveraging technology to improve daily commuting experience, reduce wait times, and promote sustainable campus mobility.
