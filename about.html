About CampusTap

CampusTap is an innovative project reimagining campus mobility through technology. Created as a thesis project by students from the University of San Carlos, it provides a cashless, real-time shuttle tracking and ride logging system.

Our Mission

To improve student transportation with real-time tracking and cashless convenience.
